About

CHIH-CHIEH HUNG

I'm an assistant professor of Tamkang University in Taiwan, an experienced data scietist and software development engineer.
I play with data: I've played a very vital role in data science team in leading e-commerce firms, built data-driven decision systems,and created value in every perspectives by using data as the tool.
